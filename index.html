Hello! This is a small project to map out various landmarks that represent Cold War politics in India.

Whenever we study about Cold War, we are mostly reminded of events unfolding between US and USSR either in Europe, Cuba, Korea or Vietnam.

Thus, this is an attempt to show how Cold War politics played out closer to home and how we were not just spectators but participants who were shaped by and also shaped the Cold War.

The map highlights statues, museums and memorials of important individuals that played a pivotal role. These sites tell a story and the hyperlinked articles will help you understand them.

For now the map only focusses on Delhi but it will be expanded to cover different sites all over the country. (Although, it is interesting to see how even in Delhi the politics was concentrated to 1 specific area.)
